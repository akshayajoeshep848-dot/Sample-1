Here are some popular stories for kids: Goldilocks and the Three Bears, The Boy Who Cried Wolf, The Tortoise and the Hare, The Little Red Hen, The Three Little Pigs, The Lion and the Mouse, The Ant and the Grasshopper, and The Fox and the Grapes. These stories are known for their engaging narratives and valuable life lessons. Here's a bit more about each story: Goldilocks and the Three Bears: A story about a girl who enters a house belonging to three bears and tries their porridge, chairs, and beds, with humorous consequences. The Boy Who Cried Wolf: A cautionary tale about the dangers of lying, where a shepherd boy's false alarms eventually lead to nobody believing him when he truly needs help. The Tortoise and the Hare: A fable about the importance of perseverance, where a slow and steady tortoise wins a race against a boastful hare.
